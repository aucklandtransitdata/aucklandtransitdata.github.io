Hack-Akl 2014 - 24-26 May 2014

Welcome to Auckland's first civic hacking event. This is an open invitation to designers, software developers, architects, or anyone who wants to be involved to participate in a weekend of 'coding for good'.

This event is what, we hope, to be first of many. It is a seed for establishing a developer community who is interested in making an ongoing contribution to open source civic software solutions that leverage open government data, be it regional or national in source.

If we are successful, not only will we have some cool technology outcomes, but more importantly, we will have formed a community within a community (Auckland and New Zealand) that is willing to own the 'civic hacking for good' agenda going forward.

Auckland Transport is taking the lead in supporting this initiative and in conjunction with Propellerhead and AUT have put this inaugural event together.

The mission of this weekend is multifaceted: Our goals include:

  • Providing an opportunity for self development and personal network building
  • Enabling innovation for greater good
  • Support the evolution and democratisation of open government data
  • Supporting the government STEM agenda - Science, Technology, Engineering and Maths
  • Creating a new Community and its self governance going forward

This event is for and about those that participate. You can choose to take a known challenge or render your own solution based on the data sets you think will bring value to the wider community in some new useful form.
